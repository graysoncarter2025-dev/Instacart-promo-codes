Quick FAQ

Q: Are Instacart promo codes always valid for every order?

A: No, codes often have restrictions, they may be limited to first orders, specific retailers, or membership accounts, so check the terms before using them.

Q: How can I safely verify a promo code before entering payment details?

A: Verify the code in the app or at checkout, confirm expiration dates, and avoid entering codes from untrusted sources that ask for login credentials or payment outside the official checkout flow.

Q: Can membership promotions stack with other discounts?

A: Sometimes, but not always, promotions and membership benefits may not stack; read the offer details and try a small test order if you are unsure.
