Quick FAQ

How do I apply an Instacart promo code?

Enter the code at checkout in the designated promo box before completing your order.

Do promo codes work for all stores on Instacart?

Some codes are store specific while others apply across multiple retailers, always check terms before using.

Are promo codes safe to use?

Yes, as long as they come from trusted sources like Instacart itself or reputable partners, avoid third party sites that look suspicious.
